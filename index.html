About My Philosophy

My work is driven by the philosophy of La Dolce Sosta—the Sweet Stop. It's about crafting moments of genuine rest, quality, and flavor, whether I'm designing a new business model, restoring an antique villa, or curating a simple wine-and-cheese pairing.

I specialize in transforming raw potential into refined, memorable experiences. From hands-on execution in agricultural sustainability to strategic planning and brand development, my focus is always on creating lasting value and deep, sensory appeal.
